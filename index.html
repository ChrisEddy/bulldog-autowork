About Us



Bulldog Autoworks Ltd. is a full mechanical repair shop, located on Jacklin Road in Langford, BC.

We also sell and install new and used tires. We can guarantee that we have the cheapest shop rate in town.

Bulldog AutoWorks Ltd. was started in 2010 by partners Laura Bull and Dave Bull. Since then, Bulldog Autoworks has blossomed into a successful shop, dedicated to quality work and friendly service.

We are fully insured and licensed to work on vehicles in the city of Langford and we offer a 1 year parts and labor warranty on all work done by our business.
